Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1 Cancellation Window

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement without any charges.

  • Cancellation requests must be submitted through your account or by contacting customer service.
  • After 24 hours, orders enter processing and may not be eligible for cancellation.
  • Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

2 Eligible Items

✓ Eligible for cancellation:

  • Standard in-stock items
  • Pre-order items (before shipping date)
  • Multiple quantity orders (partial cancellation allowed)

✗ Not eligible for cancellation:

  • Custom or made-to-order furniture
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Orders that have already shipped
  • Gift cards

3 Refund Process

Once your cancellation is confirmed:

Full refund to original payment method

Including taxes and shipping charges (if applicable)

Processing time: 5-7 business days

Refund timeline varies by payment method and bank

4 Partial Cancellation

For orders with multiple items, you may request to cancel individual items. The remaining items will continue processing normally. Shipping charges may be adjusted based on the order total after cancellation.

5 Discount Codes

If you used a discount code on your order:

  • One-time use codes may not be reusable after cancellation
  • Promotional discounts will be removed from the refund calculation
  • Contact customer service for assistance with discount codes

6 How to Cancel

Online: Go to "My Orders" and select "Cancel Order"
Phone: Call +1 (800) 123-4567 (24/7 support)
Email: cancellations@premiumliving.com
